What we need to help you
The more context you share upfront, the faster we can prioritize and show up prepared. Bring as much of this as you have — partial info is fine, none is a blocker.
CLIENT
Client & size
Account name, estimated ACV/ARR, and contract tier. Helps us calibrate effort and urgency.
PRIORITY
Priority level
Is this urgent, high, or a when-we-can? A simple signal so we can sequence the queue.
TIME
Timeline
Any deadlines, renewal dates, or client-facing milestones we need to work backward from.
ARR+
Potential ARR increase
Estimated expansion opportunity if the engagement goes well. Helps us size the investment.
RISK
Retention risk
Is churn or downgrade on the table? Flag it — risk context changes how we prioritize.
ASK
The ask
A one-line description of what you need: fix X, unblock Y, scope Z. Even rough is helpful.
When to bring us in
You're unsure, not confident, or can't find the answer in documentation — FDE is your next call.
A client's implementation is stalling, underperforming, or they want to do more with the platform.
A renewal or expansion has a technical blocker or an unanswered technical question.
You need a technical presence on a client call — kickoff, QBR, or deep-dive.
You know something needs to change but aren't sure what — bring us in to scope it together.
What we're not
A replacement for Support tickets or billing questions.
A bottleneck — if you know exactly who and what you need in Product, go direct. We're additive, not mandatory.
